Our team

The book club of SSC is one of the legendary clubs established years ago. It has been a continuously growing community where both book worms and people with no culture of reading are served. Our vision is to create a productive and essential book reading habit for our members.

Reading books and reviewing them is not the only thing we do. We’ve prepared many fun events, games and competitions as well as relevant experience sharing sessions. Some of the books we’ve read include; Turtles all the way down, Instructions for dancing, Convenience store woman and many more to come.
Join us throughout the amazing journey we’ll have this year!
